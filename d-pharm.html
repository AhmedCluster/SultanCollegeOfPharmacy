Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm)

The Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm) is a 2-year diploma program that provides foundational knowledge in pharmaceutical sciences. Our D.Pharm program at Sultan College of Pharmacy is designed to prepare students for careers in community pharmacies, hospital pharmacies, and the pharmaceutical industry.

D.Pharm Program Details

Duration: 2 years

Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics with minimum 50% marks

Intake: 60 seats

Medium of Instruction: English

Approval: Pharmacy Council of India (PCI)

Curriculum Highlights

Our D.Pharm program covers a wide range of subjects including:

First Year

  • Pharmaceutics-I
  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry-I
  • Pharmacognosy
  • Biochemistry & Clinical Pathology
  • Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Health Education & Community Pharmacy
  • Pharmaceutical Jurisprudence

Second Year

  • Pharmaceutics-II
  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry-II
  • Pharmacology & Toxicology
  • Pharmaceutical Jurisprudence & Ethics
  • Drug Store and Business Management
  • Hospital and Clinical Pharmacy

Practical Training

In addition to classroom learning, students undergo practical training in various aspects of pharmacy:

  • Dispensing of prescriptions
  • Compounding of pharmaceutical preparations
  • Identification of crude drugs
  • Chemical testing of drugs
  • Hospital pharmacy training

Career Opportunities

D.Pharm graduates have various career opportunities in:

  • Community Pharmacies
  • Hospital Pharmacies
  • Pharmaceutical Manufacturing
  • Marketing and Sales
  • Drug Distribution and Supply Chain
  • Government Health Services

Higher Education Opportunities

After completing D.Pharm, students can pursue:

  • Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) through lateral entry into the second year
  • Bachelor of Pharmacy Practice (B.Pharm Practice)

Admission Process

For detailed information about the admission process, eligibility criteria, and fee structure, please visit our Admissions page.
