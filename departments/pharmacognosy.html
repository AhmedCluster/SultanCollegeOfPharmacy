Department of Pharmacy Practice

About the Department

In the department of Pharmacy Practice the students gain skills necessary to practice pharmaceutical care upon entering the profession regardless of the practice environment with a goal to improve an individual patient's quality of life through achievement of definite, medication-related therapeutic outcomes.

The students along with pharmacy practice faculty provide clinical pharmacy services to the patients of Basaveshwara Medical College Hospital and Research Center, which is owned by our own education trust SJM Vidyapeetha Â®.
