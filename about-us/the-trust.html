SJM Vidyapeetha - The Trust

About SJM Vidyapeetha

SJM Vidyapeetha was started in the year 1966 by his holiness Jagadguru Sri Sri Mallikarjuna Murugharajendra Mahaswamiji, the then pontiff of Srimath. The present seer Sri Shivamurthy Murugha Sharanaru is the President of this Vidyapeetha under the spiritual and administrative guidance of whom about 150 institutions are running today.

These institutions along with SJM College of Pharmacy also include Medical College, Engineering College, Dental College, Nursing College, Polytechnic, Degree Colleges, High Schools, Primary and Nursery Schools, School of Dance, Drama and Music etc. Besides it has number of Hostels and Schools for physically challenged and orphan children.

To know more about SJM Vidyapeetha and Srimath, please visit www.sjmonline.org

Educational Philosophy

The trust believes in providing quality education that combines academic excellence with moral and ethical values. Our educational institutions are designed to nurture not just intellectual growth but also character development and social responsibility.

Vision of the Trust

To create centers of excellence in education that empower individuals with knowledge, skills, and values necessary to contribute meaningfully to society and lead fulfilling lives.

Mission of the Trust
  • To establish and maintain educational institutions that meet the highest standards of academic excellence
  • To provide inclusive education that reaches all sections of society
  • To promote research and innovation in various fields
  • To preserve and promote cultural heritage and values
  • To contribute to community development through education and social service
