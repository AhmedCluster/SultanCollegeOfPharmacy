B Pharm Lateral Entry

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) Lateral Entry

The B.Pharm Lateral Entry program at Sultan College of Pharmacy allows diploma holders in pharmacy to directly enter into the second year (3rd semester) of the B.Pharm program. This pathway provides an opportunity for D.Pharm graduates to continue their education and obtain a bachelor's degree in pharmacy in a shorter timeframe.

B.Pharm Lateral Entry Program Details

Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)

Eligibility: Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm) with minimum 50% marks

Intake: As per regulatory approval

Medium of Instruction: English

Approval: Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) and All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)

Curriculum

Students admitted through lateral entry join the regular B.Pharm program from the 3rd semester onwards. The curriculum includes:

Core Subjects

  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry
  • Pharmaceutics
  • Pharmacology
  • Pharmacognosy
  • Pharmaceutical Analysis
  • Pharmaceutical Biotechnology
  • Pharmaceutical Jurisprudence

Practical Training

  • Laboratory work in various pharmaceutical disciplines
  • Industrial training
  • Research projects
  • Seminar presentations

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the B.Pharm program have diverse career opportunities in:

  • Pharmaceutical Industry (Manufacturing, Quality Control, Research & Development)
  • Hospital Pharmacy
  • Community Pharmacy
  • Clinical Research
  • Regulatory Affairs
  • Marketing and Sales
  • Academic and Research Institutions

Higher Education Opportunities

After completing B.Pharm, students can pursue:

  • Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharm) in various specializations
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Pharmaceutical Management
  • Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D)
  • Ph.D in Pharmaceutical Sciences

Admission Process

For detailed information about the admission process, eligibility criteria, and fee structure, please visit our Admissions page.
