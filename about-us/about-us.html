About Us

Welcome to Sultan College of Pharmacy

Sultan College of Pharmacy is a premier institution dedicated to providing quality pharmaceutical education and research opportunities. Established in 1986, we have been at the forefront of pharmacy education in Karnataka.

Our college is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Bangalore, and is approved by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) and All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

We offer various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in pharmaceutical sciences, focusing on academic excellence, practical training, and research innovation.
