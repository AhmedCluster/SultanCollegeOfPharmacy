B.Pharm Lateral Entry

B.Pharm Lateral Entry is a direct admission to the second year of the Bachelor of Pharmacy program for D.Pharm graduates, allowing them to complete their undergraduate degree in 3 years instead of 4.

Course Overview

The B.Pharm Lateral Entry program is specifically designed for students who have completed their Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm). This pathway allows diploma holders to advance their career by obtaining a bachelor's degree in pharmacy with advanced standing. Students join directly in the second year of the B.Pharm program.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must have completed D.Pharm from a PCI approved institution
  • Minimum aggregate of 50% marks in D.Pharm (45% for SC/ST candidates)
  • Should have completed D.Pharm course within the stipulated time frame
  • Must qualify in relevant entrance examinations (NEET/State CET)
  • Age limit as per university norms

Duration & Structure

  • Duration: 3 years (6 semesters) - Direct entry to 2nd year
  • Intake: 20 seats
  • Medium of Instruction: English
  • Attendance: Minimum 80% attendance required

Curriculum

Year 2 (Entry Year)

  • Pharmaceutical Organic Chemistry
  • Physical Pharmaceutics
  • Pharmaceutical Microbiology
  • Pharmaceutical Engineering
  • Computer Applications in Pharmacy
  • Environmental Sciences

Year 3

  • Pharmaceutical Organic Chemistry
  • Medicinial Chemistry
  • Physical Pharmaceutics
  • Pharmaceutical Microbiology
  • Pharmaceutical Biotechnology
  • Pharmacognosy & Phytochemistry

Year 4 (Final Year)

  • Pharmaceutical Jurisprudence
  • Pharmaceutical Marketing
  • Pharmaceutical Biotechnology
  • Biostatistics & Research Methodology
  • Pharmacology & Toxicology
  • Novel Drug Delivery System

Additional Benefits

  • Credit transfer from D.Pharm
  • Advanced practical training
  • Industry exposure programs
  • Research project opportunities

Advantages of Lateral Entry

  • Time Saving: Complete B.Pharm in 3 years instead of 4
  • Cost Effective: Reduced total education cost
  • Career Advancement: Enhanced job opportunities
  • Higher Education: Eligibility for M.Pharm and Ph.D programs
  • Professional Growth: Better salary prospects

Career Opportunities

Upon completion, graduates have the same opportunities as regular B.Pharm graduates:

  • Pharmaceutical Industry (Production, Quality Control, R&D)
  • Hospital Pharmacy
  • Community Pharmacy
  • Drug Regulatory Affairs
  • Clinical Research
  • Pharmaceutical Marketing
  • Academia and Research
  • Government Services (Drug Inspector, etc.)

Admission Process

Admission is based on merit in the qualifying examination (D.Pharm) and performance in entrance examinations. Candidates must apply through the centralized admission process and attend counseling sessions as per university guidelines.

Quick Facts

Duration: 3 Years (Direct to 2nd Year)

Seats: 20

Eligibility: D.Pharm completed

Medium: English

Affiliation: RGUHS, Karnataka

Approval: PCI & AICTE
