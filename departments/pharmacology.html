Department of Pharmacology

About the Department

The Department of Pharmacology is dedicated to the study of drug action on biological systems and the body's response to pharmaceutical compounds. Our department plays a crucial role in training future pharmacists and researchers in the science of medicines.

We focus on providing comprehensive education in pharmacology, encompassing drug discovery, development, therapeutic applications, and safety assessment. Our faculty and students actively engage in research that contributes to the advancement of pharmaceutical sciences and patient care.
