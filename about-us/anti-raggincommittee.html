Anti Ragging Committee

Anti Ragging Policy

Sultan College of Pharmacy is committed to providing a safe and conducive learning environment for all students. The institution has a zero-tolerance policy towards ragging in any form.

The Anti Ragging Committee has been constituted as per the guidelines of the University Grants Commission (UGC) and All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) to prevent and address any incidents of ragging.

Committee Objectives:

  • To ensure a ragging-free environment in the institution
  • To create awareness about anti-ragging measures
  • To investigate complaints related to ragging
  • To recommend appropriate action against offenders

Report Ragging

If you experience or witness any form of ragging, please report immediately:

  • Contact college authorities
  • Call Anti-Ragging Helpline
  • Email: anti-ragging@sjmcp.org
  • Anonymous reporting available
