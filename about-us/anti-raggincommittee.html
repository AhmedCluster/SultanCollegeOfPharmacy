Anti Ragging Measures

Prevention Measures

  • Regular awareness programs for students
  • Display of anti-ragging notices at prominent places
  • Orientation programs for new students
  • 24/7 monitoring of hostel premises
  • Anonymous complaint system

Reporting & Action

  • Immediate investigation of complaints
  • Strict disciplinary action against offenders
  • Counseling support for affected students
  • Regular committee meetings and reviews
  • Coordination with local police if required

Important Notice

Ragging is a Criminal Offense

As per the Hon'ble Supreme Court directions and UGC regulations, ragging is completely banned. Any student found guilty of ragging will face severe punishment including expulsion from the institution.
