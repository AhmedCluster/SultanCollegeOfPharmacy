Courses Offered

SJM College of Pharmacy offers a comprehensive range of pharmacy education programs, from diploma to doctoral level. Our courses are designed to provide students with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience needed to excel in various sectors of the pharmaceutical industry and healthcare.

Course Duration Eligibility Intake Approval
Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm) 2 years 10+2 with PCB/PCM (50% min) 60 PCI
Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) 4 years 10+2 with PCB/PCM (50% min) 100 PCI, AICTE
Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D) 6 years (5+1) 10+2 with PCB (50% min) 30 PCI
Pharm.D (Post Baccalaureate) 3 years (2+1) B.Pharm (55% min) 10 PCI
M.Pharm (Pharmaceutics) 2 years B.Pharm (55% min) 15 PCI, AICTE
M.Pharm (Pharmaceutical Chemistry) 2 years B.Pharm (55% min) 15 PCI, AICTE
M.Pharm (Pharmacology) 2 years B.Pharm (55% min) 15 PCI, AICTE
Ph.D in Pharmaceutical Sciences 3-5 years M.Pharm or equivalent As per availability University
