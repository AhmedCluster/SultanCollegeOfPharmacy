Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm)

The Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) is a 4-year undergraduate degree program that provides comprehensive knowledge in pharmaceutical sciences. Our B.Pharm program at Sultan College of Pharmacy is designed to prepare students for careers in various sectors of the pharmaceutical industry, community pharmacies, hospital pharmacies, and research institutions.

B.Pharm Program Details

Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)

Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics with minimum 50% marks

Intake: 60 seats

Medium of Instruction: English

Approval: Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) and All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)

Curriculum Highlights

Our B.Pharm program covers a wide range of subjects including:

Core Subjects

  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry
  • Pharmaceutics
  • Pharmacology
  • Pharmacognosy
  • Pharmaceutical Analysis
  • Pharmaceutical Biotechnology
  • Pharmaceutical Jurisprudence
  • Medicinal Chemistry
  • Pharmacokinetics

Practical Training

  • Laboratory work in various pharmaceutical disciplines
  • Industrial training
  • Research projects
  • Seminar presentations
  • Community pharmacy training
  • Hospital pharmacy training

Program Outcomes

Upon completion of the B.Pharm program, graduates will be able to:

  • Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of pharmaceutical sciences
  • Formulate, prepare, and evaluate various pharmaceutical dosage forms
  • Understand the principles of drug action, drug metabolism, and drug interactions
  • Apply knowledge of pharmaceutical analysis for quality control and assurance
  • Understand regulatory requirements and ethical considerations in pharmacy practice
  • Develop research skills and critical thinking abilities
  • Communicate effectively with patients, healthcare professionals, and the community

Career Opportunities

B.Pharm graduates have diverse career opportunities in:

  • Pharmaceutical Industry (Manufacturing, Quality Control, Research & Development)
  • Hospital Pharmacy
  • Community Pharmacy
  • Clinical Research
  • Regulatory Affairs
  • Marketing and Sales
  • Academic and Research Institutions
  • Government Agencies

Higher Education Opportunities

After completing B.Pharm, students can pursue:

  • Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharm) in various specializations
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Pharmaceutical Management
  • Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D)
  • Ph.D in Pharmaceutical Sciences
