Goals & Objectives

Our Goals

  • To impart the job skills and research motivation in students to excel in all walks of life including the pharmaceutical sciences.
  • To impart moral values, social responsibilities and attribute of students to make them good citizens.

Educational Objectives

  • To provide comprehensive education in pharmaceutical sciences that prepares students for careers in pharmacy practice, industry, research, and academia.
  • To develop critical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and research skills in students.
  • To foster innovation and entrepreneurship in pharmaceutical sciences.
  • To promote continuous professional development and lifelong learning.

Strategic Objectives

  • Enhance academic excellence through quality teaching and learning methodologies
  • Strengthen research capabilities and infrastructure
  • Develop industry-academia partnerships
  • Promote community engagement and outreach activities
  • Ensure continuous quality improvement in all aspects of the institution

Core Values

  • Excellence in education and research
  • Integrity and ethical conduct
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Social responsibility
  • Inclusivity and diversity
  • Student-centered approach
