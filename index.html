Welcome to Sultan College of Pharmacy

About Our College

Sultan College of Pharmacy is a premier institution dedicated to providing quality pharmacy education. Established in 1986, we have been at the forefront of pharmacy education, research, and practice.

Our college offers various courses including Pharm D, B.Pharm, M.Pharm, and D.Pharm, all approved by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) and All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

Our Courses

We offer a wide range of pharmacy courses to meet the diverse needs of students

D .Pharm

Basic diploma course in pharmacy, focused on fundamentals of drugs, dispensing, and pharmacy practice.

B.Pharm

Undergraduate degree in pharmacy, covering pharmaceutical sciences, drug formulation, and clinical practice.

B.Pharm Lateral Entry

Bachelor of Pharmacy (Lateral Entry) – 3 years program Direct entry into the 2nd year of B. Pharm after completing D. Pharm.
