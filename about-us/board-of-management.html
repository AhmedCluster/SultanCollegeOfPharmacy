About the Board of Management

The Board of Management is the governing body responsible for the overall administration and strategic direction of SJM College of Pharmacy. The board consists of distinguished academicians, administrators, and professionals who bring their expertise and experience to guide the institution towards excellence in pharmacy education.

The board meets regularly to discuss and decide on important matters related to:

  • Academic policies and programs
  • Financial planning and resource allocation
  • Infrastructure development
  • Faculty recruitment and development
  • Student welfare and support services
  • Research initiatives and collaborations
  • Quality assurance and accreditation

Under the leadership of the board, SJM College of Pharmacy has achieved significant milestones in pharmacy education and research, maintaining high standards of academic excellence and professional ethics.

Roles and Responsibilities
  • Formulating policies and strategic plans for the institution
  • Ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements
  • Approving annual budgets and financial statements
  • Monitoring academic performance and quality standards
  • Establishing partnerships with industry and other educational institutions
  • Overseeing infrastructure development and maintenance
  • Promoting research and innovation in pharmacy education
