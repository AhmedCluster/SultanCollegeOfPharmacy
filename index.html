Welcome to Sultan College of Pharmacy

About Our College

Sultan College of Pharmacy is a premier institution committed to excellence in pharmaceutical education, research, and professional practice. Established in 2019, the college was founded with the vision of nurturing competent pharmacists who can contribute effectively to the healthcare sector and society.

We provide a vibrant academic environment supported by modern infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, a resourceful library, and highly qualified faculty. Our programs include Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm), Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm), and B.Pharm Lateral Entry, all approved by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI). With strong academic rigor, practical training, and industry exposure, we prepare students to become skilled healthcare professionals ready for the evolving pharmaceutical field.

Message from the President of the Trust

"It is a matter of great honor and pride to serve as the President of this Trust. Our vision is to create a center of excellence in pharmaceutical education and research, where students are equipped not only with academic knowledge but also with practical skills, ethics, and compassion to serve humanity."

The rapidly evolving field of pharmacy demands professionals who are innovative, responsible, and dedicated to the well-being of society. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a student-focused learning environment, we are committed to nurturing competent pharmacists who will contribute to the healthcare sector with integrity and excellence.

I warmly welcome you to be a part of our mission and growth.

Mohammed Abubakar

President, Administrative Committee

His Dream and Vision

The foundation of Sultan College of Pharmacy was laid under the guidance of Rahmath Education Trust with a vision rooted in the principle of "Iqra" – the importance of education for all. Dr. Sultan Salauddin believed that true education empowers individuals, strengthens families, and transforms society.

Recognizing Bidar as an educationally underserved region, his dream was to provide quality pharmacy education, especially encouraging female students under the guiding values of faith, discipline, and service to humanity. His vision was not just academic success but also building strong ethical foundations for the students.

Today, his legacy continues through the institution, inspiring students to pursue excellence with dedication, hard work, and compassion. We honor his vision by preparing competitive pharmacy professionals ready to serve mankind with integrity.

Late Dr. Sultan Salauddin

BAMS, M.S
Founder & President, Rahmath Trust
