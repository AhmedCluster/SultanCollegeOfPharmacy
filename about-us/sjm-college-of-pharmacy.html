About Sultan College of Pharmacy

Established in 2019, Sultan College of Pharmacy is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences Bangalore and Sultan College of Pharmacy. The college is recognized and approved by Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi.

The college offers a comprehensive range of pharmacy education programs bachelors and diploma With excellent infrastructure and dedicated staff, including hostel facilities, the institution provides a conducive environment for pharmacy education and research.

Many students have graduated from this institution and are serving the society in various fields including:

  • Academics
  • Drug manufacturing
  • Research and Development
  • Formulation and Development
  • Clinical Pharmacy
  • Hospital Pharmacy
  • Retail Pharmacy

Academic Excellence

The college is committed to providing quality education in pharmacy with a focus on both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Our curriculum is designed to meet the current industry requirements and prepare students for the challenges of the pharmaceutical profession.

Research Facilities

The college has established Research Centres in various disciplines including:

  • Pharmacology
  • Pharmaceutics
  • Pharmacognosy
  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry

These centers facilitate advanced research and innovation in pharmaceutical sciences.
