The Trust

SJM Vidyapeetha Trust

SJM Vidyapeetha Trust was established with the noble vision of providing quality education to students from all walks of life. The trust is committed to academic excellence and holistic development of students.

Under the guidance of the trust, various educational institutions have been established, including Sultan College of Pharmacy, which has been serving the pharmaceutical education sector since 1986.

The trust believes in creating an environment that fosters learning, research, and innovation while maintaining the highest standards of ethical and moral values.

Key Features

  • Established educational trust
  • Committed to quality education
  • Focus on holistic development
  • Emphasis on research and innovation
  • Strong ethical values
